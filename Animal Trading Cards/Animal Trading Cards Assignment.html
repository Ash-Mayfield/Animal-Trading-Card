The Grey Alpha Wolf

Wolves are best known for howling. It is a common myth that this behavior is linked to full moons...

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Size: Known to be as big as 130lbs
  • Average Lifespan: Around 15 Years in captivity and 5-6 in the wild
  • Habitat: Most comfortable in colder climates due to thick fur

The Grey Wolf is an apex predator, fierce protector, and excellent hunter. Wolves are quipped with a full coat to protect from freezing temperatures. They travel in packs and use their unique howls as a form of communcation with one another.
